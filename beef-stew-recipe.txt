Beef Stew 

Beef Roast
Carrots
Potatoes
Tomato Sauce
Onion 


Cut roast into 1 inch squares. Add to soup pot and fill with enough water to cover the meat. Dice an onion and add it to the pot to cook on high while you cut the other veggies.
Peel potatoes and cut them in about 1 inch pieces. Add them to the stew. Slice the carrots and add them. Let everything cook until veggies are desired doneness. 
Add tomato Sauce and let it boil. If the broth isn't thick enough add a little bit of flour.
